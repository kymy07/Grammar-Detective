📖 Singular & Plural Nouns

Singular noun refers to one person, place, thing or animal.

Plural noun refers to more than one.

Most nouns → add -s

cat → cats
dog → dogs
car → cars

Ends in -s/-sh/-ch/-x/-z → -es

box → boxes
brush → brushes
dress → dresses

Consonant + y → -ies

baby → babies
lady → ladies
party → parties

Irregular nouns

child → children
man → men
mouse → mice
foot → feet
