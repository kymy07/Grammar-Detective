COUNTABLE &
UNCOUNTABLE
COUNTABLE

We can use countable nouns
for things we can count.
They can be singular or plural.

1
2
EXAMPLES
SINGULARPLURAL
🐱 cat → cats
📚 book → books 📖
UNCOUNTABLE

We can use uncountable nouns
for things we cannot count.
These nouns are always singular.

3
4
EXAMPLES
1 🥛 milk
2 💧 water
3 🏖️ sand
