📖 What are Possessive Adjectives?

Possessive adjectives show ownership — they tell us who something belongs to.

Subject PronounPossessive AdjectiveExample
ImyMy sister is friendly.
YouyourCan I borrow your pencil?
HehisHis bike is yellow.
SheherHer dress is pink.
ItitsThe tree lost its leaves.
WeourWe love our school.
TheytheirTheir car is new.
