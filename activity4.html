📖 What are Personal Pronouns?

A personal pronoun replaces the name of a person, place or thing. There are 12 personal pronouns: I, you, he, she, it, we, they, me, him, her, us, them.

PersonSingular (Subject)Plural (Subject)Object
1stIweme / us
2ndyouyouyou
3rdhe / she / ittheyhim / her / it / them

Examples:

I love playing games! She is a pilot. She gave me a chocolate. The teacher gave us a lollipop.
✏️ Exercise — He, She or It?
Use He for a boy · She for a girl · It for a thing or animal.
